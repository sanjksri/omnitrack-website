Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about easyomnitracker.in

Why choose EasyOmniTracker?

As the name suggests, it is easy, omni-present, and reliable tracking. Any non-technical person can set it up within a few clicks. There is no external hardware needed, no app download required, and it works seamlessly on any smartphone with minimal registration requirements.

What about user privacy?

Since this is a dedicated device tracking application, location tracking is unavoidable when active. However, to safeguard your identity, no personal information (such as names, phone numbers, or personal IDs) is collected or stored, meaning there is zero risk of personal profile misuse. Furthermore, explicit consent must be provided by checking the consent checkbox before location tracking begins, and drivers can stop tracking at any time.

Why use this instead of an AirTag or JioTag?

Consumer tags are designed for personal use and must be paired to a specific phone without robust historical data. For any serious business application, you need simultaneous multi-device tracking, centralized management, and historical backups.

How long is my data stored?

We securely store your historical location data for up to 6 months. You can easily download your data in CSV format at any time for your own analytics and business operations.

What are the common use cases?

Our platform is highly versatile. It is actively used for monitoring commercial fleets, delivery staff, security guards, on-field sales teams, and more.

What if my staff changes? Can they still log data?

Technically, yes, if they retain the access link. However, nobody gains from logging their personal location data. For complete security, you can easily change the unique "passcode" for that tracking ID from your dashboard, instantly revoking their access.

Is there a referral program?

Yes! Our "Refer & Earn" program allows you to earn a generous 30% commission on the payments made by anyone you refer, up to a maximum of ₹1500 per referral.

Web App vs. Native Android App?

If drivers are willing to keep the web application open on their screen during transit, there is no need to download the Android app. However, if they need to minimize the browser, run other utilities, or keep their screen turned off, they must download and install the dedicated Android App (APK) from their driver portal to prevent tracking from pausing.
