Privacy Policy

Application: Easy Omni Tracker / OmniTrack Driver (com.easyomnitracker.app)

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

Notice regarding Location Tracking: Easy Omni Tracker is a dedicated fleet and device location tracking application. Real-time location data is collected only with explicit user consent. Tracking can be paused or stopped by the user at any time directly within the application.

1. Introduction

Welcome to easyomnitracker.in ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Easy Omni Tracker / OmniTrack Driver (Package Name: com.easyomnitracker.app) and web application collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide accurate real-time tracking, fleet management, and analytics, we collect the following types of information:

A. Location Data (Foreground & Background)

  • Precise Location Data: GPS coordinates, speed, heading, and timestamp.
  • Background Location: If enabled and consented by the user, the app collects location data in the background (even when closed or not in use) to enable continuous fleet tracking, route history, and mileage reporting during active tracking sessions.

B. Device & Technical Data

  • Device identifiers (Unique Device ID / Android ID).
  • Device status information (Battery level, charging state, network connectivity status, OS version).

C. Account Information

  • Account credentials (Email address, phone number, vehicle identifier / driver ID).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for legitimate operational purposes, including:

  • Providing real-time device location monitoring and fleet visualization for account managers.
  • Calculating trip distances, routes, speed logs, and operational reports.
  • Alerting managers or drivers in case of status updates (e.g., low battery, offline status).
  • Ensuring app stability, security, bug fixes, and performance optimization.

4. User Consent and Privacy Controls

Your privacy and control are paramount:

  • Explicit Authorization: Tracking cannot occur without explicit user login and permission grants on the device.
  • User Control: Drivers can start, pause, or stop location tracking at any time using the main toggle inside the app.
  • Transparent Notifications: A persistent foreground notification is displayed on the device whenever active tracking is running.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or location data to third parties or advertisers.

Data is shared only with:

  • Authorized Account Owners: Managers and fleet administrators associated with your organization or account.
  • Service Providers: Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., Firebase / Google Cloud) strictly to host and run our backend services under confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Security & Encryption

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between the app and server is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Stored data is protected using secure database security rules and access controls.

7. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

We retain location and trip logs only for as long as necessary to fulfill fleet management reporting requirements. You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated location history.

To request data deletion, please contact us at: easyomnitracker@gmail.com with your registered account details. We will process your request within 7 business days.
